Google XX+UX

Designer and Project Coordinator

Google XX+UX is a community of women in UX who support each other at all career stages.

As the community expanded beyond San Francisco, the founding team at Google wanted promotional swag to be designed by members of the community and distributed at chapter events around the world. I was the project coordinator and one of the designers. In addition to design, I was also responsible for keeping the project on schedule; collecting and organizing assets via Google Docs; keeping meeting minutes; and liasing communication between the designers, who were based remotely around the world, and the XX+UX team at Google Mountain View. You can grab the postcards and stickers we designed at an XX+UX event near you.
